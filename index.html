Hi, there!

I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student with a near-perfect GPA of 19.29/20 at Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT) , one of Iran's top universities.
I thrive at the intersection of theory and innovation and passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI especially in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Currently, I'm exploring human identification systems. My research also spans multimodal AI, where I explore tools for Persian-language animation generation using models like Midjourney and Runway.

My academic journey began at the National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents (NODET) in Tabriz, where I graduated with a perfect 19.99/20 in Mathematical Physics and reflected by my national entrance rank of 143 out of 43,946. From an early age, my fascination with mathematics evolved into a love for computer science, where logic and creativity combine seamlessly.
When I'm not training models or optimizing algorithms, I contribute as a Teaching Assistant for courses like Advanced Programming and mentor peers in tackling complex problems. Let's connect and collaborate on the next breakthrough!
